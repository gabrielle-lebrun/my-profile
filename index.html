Hello! I'm Gabrielle Lebrun, also known as "Mon cher Guy".

Mon cher Guy is a DJ/producer who stands out for her retro-melodic style with an indie dance sound; a musical genre that oscillates between the nostalgia of post-disco, the melodic structure of alternative/indie rock, and the contemporary energy of dance.

Her love of music began at an early age, as a clarinet player. Today, Mon cher Guy strives to democratize the indie dance genre around a sunny, energetic atmosphere.

Highlights from 2023

In 2023, she played over forty shows, including the Rex Club, Badaboum, Marvellous Island festival, Cocorico Electro... Her latest track, "Malfunction", is out on the Swedish label Club Mackan, and her next EP will be released this autumn.
